About the Campaign

The year is 2185 on a planet called Eden-5. The other 4 Edens were destroyed during the War of Greed in 2172 with the Mega Corporations. It was a deadly war, with robots fighting for the Corpos and everyone else fighting for independence. There's a faction of robot resistance fighters called Lord of Blades that despised the Corpos, that are still around today in the city of Harko. There's remnants of old technology still active since the war. There's law and order for now. In the upper levels, the rich and the 1% live happily in walled off estates while everyone else ventures freely around the city. The lower levels are where criminals and low-lives tend to cause chaos and disruption with law-makers called The Peacekeepers. Pollution and toxic waste linger true the streets and people wear masks or have purifiers to get by. It's a steampunk city with industrialism architecture and everyone has a neurolink that was implanted in them since birth. With the neurolink everyone can access computers and any other electronics in a 20 foot radius. The easiest way to access the lower levels is a mechanism in the centre of the city. Gangs, murderers, cannibals, cultists, mutants and a dark force is waiting for you to adventure out and stop them from making things worse.
